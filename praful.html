Journey Of Life

         Born in small Town Hinganghat. Having illiterate family background but still wanted to be literate. But due to poor family condition was unable to spend money on education. So he started Newspaper distribution and Milk distribution work for money. After that moved to Wardha for B.Sc. Agriculture course and got job in Ghatanji. After working for 2 yrs, joined another company in Katol dist. But he wont like private companies so he left his job to prepare for Gov. jobs. After studying for almost 4 yrs he has been placed in Uttarakhand Gramin Bank as Agriculture Field Officer. During this 4 years he failed many times, but his hard work, confidence and support from his younger brother helped him to achieve this.
